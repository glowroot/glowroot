Chart navigation tips
  • drag or double click to zoom in
  • use ctrl + scroll wheel to zoom in and out
Chart vs breakdown
  • the chart stacks each timer's self time (exclusive time, not including nested timers); the stack height is the average response time
  • the breakdown total column is inclusive average time (parents include nested timers); use the self column to compare with chart segments. Inclusive rows do not need to add up to the chart total
